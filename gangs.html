Crew

This status is for new organised crime groups to start building. You will need to follow your 5 member cap in order to apply for a Gang/ Crew Status. Once approved you move onto Unverified

  • 🚫 No clothing
  • 🚫 Cannot contest colors or turf
  • 🚫 No perks

Unverified

A gang that has applied and been accepted.

  • 🚫 Cannot contest colors or turf
  • 👕 Generic clothing
  • 🚫 No perks

Trial Gang

Unlock initial gang perks.

  • 👕 6x Clothing Items
  • 🔥 RBRP Official Gang RP.
  • 🚗 Access to gang vehicle sales
  • 🏠 Garage in your area

Official Organisation

Become a recognized crime entity.

  • 👕 8x Clothing Items
  • 🔥 RBRP Official Gang RP.
  • 🛒 Gang black market access
  • 🚗 Gang Vehicles Sales
  • 🏠 Gang Warehouse
  • 🎨 Custom spray paint Logo

Redback Verified Organisation

The highest level of organized crime.

  • 👕 10x Clothing Items
  • 🔥 RBRP Official Gang RP.
  • 🛒 RB Tier 2 Shop access
  • 🚗 Gang Vehicles Sales
  • 🏠 Gang MLO, Warehouse & Garage
  • 🎨 Custom spray paint Logo

Gang Progression & Perks

This status is for new organised crime groups to start building...

  • 🚫 No clothing
  • 🚫 Cannot contest colors or turf
  • 🚫 No perks

Unverified is the status a gang receives when accepted by Gang Staff.

  • 🚫 Cannot contest colors or turf
  • 👕 Can wear generic Redback clothing (flannels, masks etc)
  • 🚫 No perks

As a trial gang, you unlock additional gang RP and perks.

  • 👕 6x Clothing Items
  • 🚗 Access to gang vehicle sales
  • 🏠 Garage in your desired area

Official organisations gain access to advanced gang operations.

  • 👕 8x Clothing Items
  • 🚗 Access to gang vehicle sales
  • 🚧 Access to gang black market
  • 🏠 Access to Gang Warehouse
  • 🎨 Custom Logo in spray paint

The elite level of organised crime within RedbackRP.

  • 🔥 10x Clothing Items
  • 🚗 Access to gang vehicle sales
  • 🎨 Custom Logo in spray paint
  • 🏠 Access to Gang MLO & MLO garage
  • 🛒 Access to Redback Tier 2 Shop
